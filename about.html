About FriendlyBattleGames

Learn more about our mission, history, and the team behind the games.

Our Purpose

We aim to inspire influencers and online personalities who stream video games to compete with each other in a safe, fair, and respectful environment. We believe competition can foster community when approached with the right mindset.

Our History

FriendlyBattleGames began in 2020 with a simple goal: to show battle game competitors a new way to engage. We envisioned a space where the friendly bonds built *after* a match are just as important as the competition itself. We host battles and tournaments focused on this friendly environment, asking all participants to embrace our rules and concept. Our commitment is to ensure every event upholds safety and respect, building a positive battle game community together.

About the Organization

FriendlyBattleGames is an original battle game event organizer representing a diverse community including online personalities, streaming platforms, gamers, athletes, and influencers who play for fun and camaraderie. Explore our consumer clothing store and join our organized battle game events and tournaments designed for friendly competition.

About the Team

Our team is composed of passionate influencers, dedicated online personalities, experienced organizers, and engaging hosts. We actively stream, download, and participate in battle games, join global tournaments, and enjoy playing with friends. We stream for fun but always bring a competitive edge, embodying the spirit of FriendlyBattleGames.
